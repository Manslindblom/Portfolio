About

I’m a design student from Hälsingland, Sweden. I’m currently studying UX/UI design at IT-Högskolan in Gothenburg. As a former construction worker and proffesional drummer, i’ve always been a creative person who appreciates details and aestetics. After many years working as a social worker, i’ve learned to always put humans and their individual needs first. And now, as a design student, i get to combine these characteristics in natural and fluent way.
